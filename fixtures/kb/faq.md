# FAQ

**Q: What is the wifi password in the Jakarta office?**
A: Network "HQ-Staff", password rotates monthly — check the poster in the kitchen.

**Q: How do I get a monitor or other equipment?**
A: File a request in #it-helpdesk. Budget is one external monitor per person.

**Q: Who approves vacation requests?**
A: Your direct manager, through the HR portal. Requests need 2 weeks notice for
trips longer than 3 days.

**Q: What's the on-call compensation?**
A: One paid day off per week of on-call rotation, taken within the next quarter.
