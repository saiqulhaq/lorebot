# Onboarding

Welcome to the team! Your first-week checklist:

1. Get your laptop from IT (room 3B, ask for Dewi).
2. Request access to GitHub, Slack, and the VPN via the #it-helpdesk channel.
3. The staging environment credentials live in 1Password under "Staging Shared".
4. Book a 30-minute intro call with your buddy — HR assigns one on day 1.

Payroll runs on the 25th of each month. Expense reports go to finance@example.com
and are reimbursed within two weeks.
